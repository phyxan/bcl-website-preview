Common Questions

Before you call, you probably want to know

How much does a criminal defense lawyer cost in Dallas?

Most cases are handled on a flat fee that depends on the charge, the court, and whether trial is likely. Misdemeanors generally cost less than felonies, and federal cases cost more than state cases. The initial case review is free, so you will know what your case involves before you spend anything.

What should I do right after an arrest in Dallas?

Stay calm, say that you are choosing to remain silent, and ask for a lawyer. Do not explain your side to police, do not consent to searches, and do not discuss your case on jail phones. They are recorded. Then contact a defense attorney quickly. Some deadlines start immediately, including the 15-day license hearing deadline in DWI cases.

Does Peter handle both state and federal charges?

Yes. Peter has defended clients in Texas state courts and in federal courts, including the Northern District of Texas, for 31 years. The systems run on different rules and different sentencing structures, and experience in both matters.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes. The initial case review is free and confidential. You tell us what happened, and we explain what you are facing and what your options are. There is no obligation to hire the firm.
