About the Attorney

Peter M. Barrett

A top-ranked trial lawyer, Mr. Barrett has successfully handled thousands of criminal cases since 1994. He built his practice on a simple promise: the person who answers your questions, prepares your motions, and stands beside you at trial is the lawyer you hired.

Peter focuses his practice entirely on criminal defense, in Texas state courts and in federal court. Over 31 years he has taken more than 100 cases to a jury verdict, a number most defense lawyers never reach. That experience changes how prosecutors negotiate, because they know which lawyers will actually try a case.

His peers elected him President of the Dallas Criminal Defense Lawyers Association in 2005 after five years on its board. His writing on Texas evidence law has appeared in Voice for the Defense, the journal of the Texas Criminal Defense Lawyers Association. And his practice is bilingual: clients and their families work with him in English or Spanish.

Philosophy

You are a person here, not a case number

Most people who call this office have never been in trouble before. They are scared, their family is scared, and the system is moving fast in a language nobody taught them. Peter built the firm around that reality.

You will not be passed between associates. You will not wonder what is happening with your case. Phone calls get returned. Strategy gets explained before it happens, in plain English or Spanish, so every decision is one you actually made.

And every case gets prepared the same way: as if it is going to trial. Cases prepared for trial settle better, get dismissed more often, and win more verdicts when they do not settle. That has been the approach for 31 years, and it is the reason former clients send their friends and family here.
